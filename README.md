# Marketing-Analytics

# 📊 Marketing Analytics for an E-Commerce Marketplace

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing end-to-end marketing analytics for an e-commerce marketplace to uncover actionable business insights. By analyzing customer, seller, product, payment, and order data, the project enables data-driven decision-making to evaluate business performance, identify growth opportunities, and optimize marketing strategies.

---

## 📂 Dataset Overview

The analysis is built using multiple interconnected datasets covering:

* Transactions & Orders
* Payments
* Customers
* Sellers
* Products & Categories
* Locations

These datasets were integrated to provide a comprehensive view of the marketplace ecosystem.

---

## 🛠 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Cleaned missing and inconsistent data.
* Standardized data formats and merged multiple datasets.
* Performed preprocessing to ensure accurate and reliable analysis.

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Evaluated key business metrics such as revenue, orders, customers, and sellers.
* Analyzed customer acquisition and retention trends.
* Identified top-performing products and categories based on sales performance.

### 3. Trend & Seasonality Analysis

* Examined sales and order quantity trends across:

  * Product categories
  * Months
  * Weeks
  * Days
  * Hours
* Identified seasonal demand patterns and purchasing behavior over time.

### 4. Customer & Seller Segmentation

* Segmented customers and sellers based on their purchasing and selling behavior.
* Highlighted high-value segments to support targeted marketing and business strategies.

### 5. Cross-Selling Analysis

* Identified products that are frequently purchased together.
* Uncovered opportunities for product bundling and personalized recommendations.

### 6. Payment Behavior Analysis

* Analyzed preferred payment methods.
* Studied payment trends and regional variations in customer payment preferences.

### 7. Customer Satisfaction Analysis

* Evaluated customer ratings and reviews across different products and categories.
* Identified factors influencing customer satisfaction and overall shopping experience.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* Discovered seasonal demand patterns across multiple product categories.
* Identified customer segments with strong retention and repeat purchase behavior.
* Revealed cross-selling opportunities through frequently co-purchased products.
* Observed regional differences in customer payment preferences.
* Evaluated product and category performance using customer ratings and reviews to identify improvement opportunities.
